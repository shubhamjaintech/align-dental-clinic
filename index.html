Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and dental care.

How often should I visit the dentist?

Most patients should visit the dentist every six months for a check-up and cleaning. However, Dr. Anjali may recommend more frequent visits based on your specific oral health needs.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major dental insurance plans. Please contact our office with your insurance information before your appointment so we can verify your coverage.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, debit cards, and UPI payments. We also offer flexible payment plans for more extensive treatments.

How can I manage dental anxiety?

At Align Dental Clinic, we understand dental anxiety is common. Dr. Anjali specializes in treating anxious patients with a gentle approach. We offer comfort amenities, clear communication, and sedation options when necessary.

What should I do in a dental emergency?

Contact our office immediately at +91 78990 97709. We reserve time in our schedule for emergency appointments. For after-hours emergencies, our answering service will direct you to emergency care.
